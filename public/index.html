About MintBot

MintBot allows you to utilize a JSON-parsed version of your resume to have a chat bot speak to recruiters and potential employers. MintBot takes on your persona, and will converse with recruiters to provide specific information taken from your resume.

Technologies used to create MintBot:
  • JavaScript
  • HTML5
  • CSS
  • SASS
  • JSON Resume
  • Botkit.ai
Mintbot was created using Botkit.ai, an open source developer tool for building chat bots, apps and custom integrations, throughout hack-a-thon organized by Mintbean. Mintbean provides a dev-driven community to stay active and engaged within the software development industry.
